Inspiration

Read below how the idea of Alyce was conceived.

A good screenplay is one of the most important aspects of a great movie. Viewers should be able to relate and be part of the emotional ride the scene takes them through. For a person to be able to write a good screenplay, they will need years of experience and immense talent. Those with the required experience and talents are not always available. Stories can be viewed as an unstructured collection of information about an event while screenplays are properly structured and well defined. Stories don’t expand the background details and also describe the scenes vaguely. They don’t paint the picture quite well for a movie. Stories just form the basis for a screenplay but the actual details are further added or modified by the writers.

“Alyce Garner Peterson” (or “Alyce”), is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) fine-tuned Screenplay Writer who generates the screenplay from a given story using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning techniques. Alyce achieves what other writers strive to achieve in less time.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have listed some frequent questions which we hear from eager users like you and have posted it's answers

  • Who is Alyce Garner Peterson?

    Alyce Garner Peterson is A.I fine tuned screenplay writer. Alyce generates a screenplay from a story/novel. Alyce Garner Peterson is also an anagram for the word "Screenplay Generator"

  • Does Alyce generate any Visualisaton?

    Alyce is just a screenplay writer and do not create visuals of any sort for atleast now!

  • How can I know more about its implemenation?

    To know more about how Alyce works, please do visit the GitHub repository. Maybe even send a pull request if you think you can contribute to Alyce!

  • How to see a demo of Alyce in action?

    We've embedded a video within this page or just click on "Get Started".

  • How is the voice generated in Alyce?

    Alyce currently uses the MozillaTTS engine with the vocoder as WaveRNN.

  • How is the plot generated?

    Alyce with the help of the Plug and Play Language Model and user interaction, generates the required plot.

  • Why is there a user interaction during plot generation?

    Sometimes users may want the plot according to their imagination. Alyce is designed to consider such imaginations too. User interaction is completely optional if the plot generated by Alyce is satisfactory.
